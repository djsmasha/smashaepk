About

DJ SMASHA is a producer and open format DJ from the DMV. He has been producing for 18 years and DJing for 14. SMASHA's versatility spans across multiple genres, including Hip-hop, Afrobeats, Amapiano, House, Electronic and more.

He has DJed many venues in the DMV area from U Street Music Hall to Decades to Saint Ex to Mayflower to Flash among many others. SMASHA has also gained recognition for his edits and remixes.

His SoundCloud has amassed almost 900,000 total streams with remixes that have been supported from well-known DJs like Diplo, DJ Puffy, Ape Drums, SpydaTEK, Four Color Zack and many more. His tracks have also been featured on various DJ pools, such as DJ City and BPM Supreme.
